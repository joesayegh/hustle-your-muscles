Cohen

"What seems impossible today will soon become a distant memory."

Despite a successful commercial career building and mentoring market-leading teams, Paul decided a change was needed — so he did something about it. He retrained as a Personal Trainer and Coach and never looked back.

Paul found new reward in helping others achieve healthier and happier lifestyles. His specialisms include Strength Training, Programme Design, Body Conditioning, Youth Fitness, TRX and Functional Movement.

A husband and busy father of three — if Paul has time to look after himself, so do you.
